IIT Bombay is a public technical and research university located in Powai, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It is one of the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. IIT Bombay was established in 1958 and has since become one of the premier engineering institutions in India, known for its academic excellence, research contributions, and vibrant campus life.

IIT Bombay offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines, as well as in sciences, humanities, and management. The institute is renowned for its rigorous academic curriculum, world-class faculty, and state-of-the-art research facilities. IIT Bombay has consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions in India and has gained international recognition for its contributions to research and innovation. The campus is also known for its vibrant student community, cultural events, and entrepreneurial activities.

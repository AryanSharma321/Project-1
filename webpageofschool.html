Sanskriti School is a co-educational day school located in the heart of New Delhi, India. Established in 2003, the school has been committed to providing quality education and fostering holistic development among its students. With a focus on academic excellence, extracurricular activities, and character building, Sanskriti School aims to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the modern world. The school offers a wide range of subjects and encourages students to explore their interests and talents through various clubs and sports activities. With a dedicated faculty and a supportive learning environment, Sanskriti School continues to be a leading institution in the field of education in India.
